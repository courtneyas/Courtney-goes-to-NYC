But why New York?

Chicago has been an amazing experience and I've spent six wonderful years here. I want to move to NYC to live outside of the midwest for the first time in my life. I want to live nearer to a coast instead of the great lakes. I grew up in the middle of nowhere and in high school I always felt like "making it" would mean living in NYC. Part of me has never let that dream go. I've had close calls with moving the NYC before and it hasn't quite happened and it feels like I'm at the place now where I could finally find a job and make it happen. It feels like the right time to make the leap is finally here!
